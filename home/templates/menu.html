About US

Yummy Yard is a modern online food ordering platform designed exclusively for college campuses. It offers a convenient way for students to order meals from various campus dining options and get them delivered hot and fresh to their dorms,study areas,or any on-campus location. Tailored to meet the specific needs of college students, Yummy Yard simplifies campus dining.
